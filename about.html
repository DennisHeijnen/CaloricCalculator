This tool is developed for personal trainers to calculate the optimal caloric intake for their clients. The formula used in this calculator is scientifically validated and presented in the Henselmans PT Course.

Individual athletes are ofcourse allowed to make use of the calculator. However, it should be noted that one should have extensive knowledge about the different variables used in this calculator. Moreover, it might be hard to be objective about one's own state of physique. Therefore, we recommend to let a certified personal trainer use the calculator for you.
